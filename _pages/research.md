My research interests are broadly in safe learning, control, and online decision-making. I am especially interested in algorithms that learn from data while maintaining constraint satisfaction in safety-critical systems.

## Safe online optimization and learning

I study online optimization and bandit learning problems in which decisions must satisfy safety constraints while learning from feedback. This includes constrained online convex optimization, safe bandits, and pricing mechanisms for network resource allocation.

## Safe and adaptive control

I am interested in control algorithms that provide performance guarantees while respecting state and input constraints. My recent work includes online nonstochastic control with convex safety constraints and related problems in constrained adaptive control.

## Energy systems and smart grids

I work on learning and control methods motivated by energy systems, including pricing-based resource allocation, smart grid applications, and stochastic model predictive control with deep forecasting models.
