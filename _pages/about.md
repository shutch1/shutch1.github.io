I am a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California, Santa Barbara, advised by Prof. Mahnoosh Alizadeh. My research focuses on safe learning and control with smart grid applications. In particular, This includes work on online optimization, bandit learning and adaptive control, as well as applications to pricing design in the electric grid.

I am also fortunate to have done an internship at Mitsubishi Electric Research Laboratories (MERL) in summer 2025, where I worked on stochastic model predictive control with deep forecasting models for energy systems.
